Your Strategic Finance Partner

FP&A & Strategic Finance

Scaling fast is exciting, until the numbers stop making sense. I step in as your fractional FP&A partner to bring structure, visibility, and control to your finances. Budgeting, rolling forecasts, board decks, KPI frameworks, revenue analysis, sales & revenue optimization, track revenue opportunities, cost optimization. Everything your leadership team needs to make smart decisions, fast.

Business Plans & Pitch Decks

Raising your first or second round? I build your financial story from scratch: a solid business plan, a defensible financial model, and a pitch deck that speaks the language investors expect. From assumptions to projections, I make sure your numbers tell a compelling and credible story.

Accounts Payable Management

Full AP cycle management for high-growth tech companies. Vendor onboarding, invoice processing, payment runs, reconciliations, and system integrations (NetSuite, Tipalti, Sage Intacct). Reliable, audit-ready financial operations that scale with the business.

M&A & Acquisition Readiness

Preparing your company for acquisition or due diligence. Multi-year financial assembly (P&L, BS, CF), EBITDA reconciliation schedules, add-back documentation, owner compensation normalization, and buyer-ready packages that hold up under QoE scrutiny.

About Us

Finance That Moves as Fast as You Do

At BoWave, we believe finance should drive growth, not slow it down. We combine deep FP&A expertise with modern tools and hands-on partnership to give startups and growing companies the financial clarity they need to move fast and make smart decisions.

Do More With Less

We don't believe in throwing hours at a problem. We believe in working smart, using AI tools, automation, and modern workflows to deliver better results in less time.

Never on Autopilot

Side by Side With Leadership

Always Evolving
